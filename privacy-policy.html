Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Super LTC ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome extension that provides clinical productivity tools for long-term care professionals using PointClickCare. This policy explains what data we access, why, and how it is handled.

What Data We Access

  • PointClickCare session cookies — We read the last_org cookie on pointclickcare.com to identify which facility you are working in. This is required to route API requests to the correct facility context.
  • Authentication token — When you log in to Super LTC, an authentication token is stored locally in Chrome's storage on your device. This token is never shared with third parties.
  • Page context — We detect which PointClickCare page is active (e.g., patient chart, facility dashboard) to show the relevant tools. No page content is transmitted unless you explicitly trigger a query.
  • Query inputs — When you submit a clinical query or use the AI chat, your input is sent to the Super LTC API at superltc.com to generate a response. These requests are authenticated and transmitted over HTTPS.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect, store, or sell patient health information (PHI).
  • We do not track browsing activity outside of pointclickcare.com.
  • We do not share any data with third-party advertisers or analytics services.
  • We do not access any websites other than pointclickcare.com and superltc.com.

Data Storage

Your authentication token and user preferences are stored locally in Chrome's storage.local on your device. This data is not synced to Chrome's cloud sync and is not accessible to other extensions or websites.

Data Retention

Locally stored data (authentication token, preferences) is cleared when you log out or uninstall the extension. Query logs on our servers are retained for up to 90 days for debugging and service improvement purposes, then permanently deleted.

HIPAA Notice

Super LTC is designed for use in healthcare settings. We do not store PHI on our servers. Clinical queries you submit are processed transiently and are not associated with patient identifiers in our systems. You are responsible for ensuring your use of this extension complies with your facility's policies and applicable regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the extension after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
